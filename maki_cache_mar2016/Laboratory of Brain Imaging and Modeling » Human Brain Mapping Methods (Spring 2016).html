Human Brain Mapping Methods (Spring 2016)

INTRO
This course focus on the introduction of modern non-invasive human neuroimaging facilities, including but not limited to, structural magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI), MR spectroscopic imaging, magnetocephalography (MEG), and transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS). We will also include a very brief introduction to the cellular and system level neuroscience before presenting individual imaging methods. We wish to bring you a general concept of neuroimaging methods as well as their features, advantages, and limitations.
